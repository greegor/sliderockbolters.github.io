Our Story

Alixe Landry got involved in the House of Rock at Swallow Hill, where she excelled on the bass. Alixe's love of funk, R&B and soul shows in her powerful and groovy bass lines.

Mamma Bear

Alixe struck out on her own and established the band in 2018, with the goal of forging a happy family of great musicians to make great music and bring that happiness to everyone who comes to our shows.
